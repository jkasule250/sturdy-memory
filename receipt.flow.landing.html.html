Common challenges

What businesses
struggle with

Every lost receipt is a potential tax headache. Here's what we see small businesses dealing with daily.

Missing receipts

Receipts get lost in wallets, bags, and inboxes — only found once it's too late to claim them.

Manual record keeping

Entering expenses by hand is error-prone, tedious, and doesn't survive any growth in transaction volume.

Tax preparation stress

Scrambling through shoeboxes of paper in the weeks before a tax deadline is a familiar nightmare for SMBs.

Audit preparation

Proving every expense under scrutiny requires documentation that most businesses simply don't maintain.

Disorganised expenses

Without a clear system, spotting where money is going — or catching duplicate charges — is nearly impossible.
